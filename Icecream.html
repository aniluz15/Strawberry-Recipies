Strawberry Cookies

Home

The ingredients include all purpose flour, baking powder, and salt (salt is critical to activate the baking powder), granulated and brown sugar, eggs, vanilla extract, lemon juice and strawberries.

First, chop up the strawberries and soak them in lemon juice and sugar

Next, prepare the dough and fold in strawberries

Then, scoop out medium sized balls and bake them, spaced about 2" apart on a large tray at 350F.

Finally Enjoy! :D
